About me

Hi! I'm Jerry LACMOU ZEUTOUO, postdoctoral researcher at the INRIA Lyon Center in Laboratoire de l'Informatique du Parallélisme, France. I am working on a component-based programming model for HPC applications in the the joint project-team Avalon INRIA.

Before this:

  • I am a part-time lecturer at the University Claude Bernard Lyon 1 from October 2022 until now.
  • I was research engineer at the the INRIA Lyon Center from October 2022 until 31 December 2023.
  • I received a Ph.D. in parallel computing at the University of Dschang in 2022.
  • I am a part-time lecturer at the Institut Universitaire et Stratégique de l’Estuaire (IUEs/Insam) from Febuary 2021 until April 2021.
  • I am a monitor at the University of Dschang from October 2018 until July 2021.
  • I received a master's degree in computer science specializing in networks and distributed services from University of Dschang in 2017.
  • I received a bachelor's degree in fundamental computer science at the University of Dschang in 2015.
